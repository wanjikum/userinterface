MIT Admission Essay

This post is written by Lawrence

I am a sixteen year old student who is highly fascinated by math and physics. My love for the pure sciences has grown since I was a little child. I have always been an active participant of science projects and activities throughout school. In fact, I have had very few summer holidays because I spend most of the holiday time participating in summer math camps. I am now applying to join MIT because I would love to pursue a degree in mathematics. I feel that this is the field I am most familiar with and that can help me best express my strengths.

As a young person who loves to be busy and productive, I do work as a tutor for young children in school. I love to help the children handle the academic challenges they face in class as well as to help me make an extra buck. Additionally, I do collect math problem sets and books for the children. I have worked as a counselor for older students who want to get information on subjects such as physics, math, informatics and chemistry. I have also been a judge on the math Olympiad for young students at my school besides attending numerous math and IT conferences.
